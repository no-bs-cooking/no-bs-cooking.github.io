Beefburger (In N Out Style) by Joshua Weissman
1
  • 500 g high-fat minced beef
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder


MIX & FORM
2
  • patties
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil


SEASON & FRY
3
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • fresh thyme
  • 2 garlic cloves (halved)


BASTE
4
  • brioche buns
  • 2 tsp butter


TOAST
5
  • white onion (sliced)


FRY
6
  • cooked patties
  • cheese slices
  • cooked onion slices


COVER & ASSEMBLE
7
  • patty ensemble
  • toasted buns
  • burger sauce
  • tomato (sliced)
  • lettuce (sliced)
